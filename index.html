About Us

Our Mission

We work together to improve the lives of women and families in Mukono District through farming, crafts, and health education.

Our History

Kyankima Women's Cooperative was started in 2019 by 47 women in Mukono District. We began with word of mouth and community radio. Today we have a website to reach more customers and share our work.

Health Tips

Every month, three health workers share tips to help our members and community stay healthy.

Florence Nakato — Community Nurse, Mukono HC III — 04/2026

Clean Water This Season

With the rain season here, open water can get dirty. Always boil water before drinking. If a child has diarrhoea, use oral rehydration salts (ORS) and visit a health centre if it continues for more than 24 hours.

Dr. Samuel Waiswa — Clinical Officer, Mukono District Hospital — 04/2026

Malaria Prevention

Sleep under a treated mosquito net every night. Clear stagnant water near your home. If you have fever or chills, go for a malaria test. Do not use leftover medicine.

Prossy Nansubuga — Nutritionist — 04/2026

Eating Well on a Small Budget

You do not need expensive food to eat well. Nakati, beans, and sweet potatoes give your body what it needs. Eat a mix of orange, green, and white foods each day.
